OnTheGo Coffee

Branding and UI design work for OnTheGo Coffee mobile cafe based in South Africa, Gauteng. OnTheGo Coffee is a mobile cafe that roams around office parks around Gauteng cities delivering hot and iced coffee in various flavors, operates on weekdays.

I designed a logo, social media post, and UI App that can be downloaded on IOS and Android mobile phones that communicates their message well and easily understandable.
