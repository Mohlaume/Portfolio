Petra Natural

Petra Natural skin care products have several benefits over synthetic or chemical-based products. Natural skin care products are made from ingredients that are gentle on the skin and do not cause irritation or other adverse reactions.

This is why I incorporated shades of nature with a minimalist serif typeface to highlight and emphasize how organic products are to one's skin.
